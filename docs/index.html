Assignment 1: Hero

  • Hero WebGL

    • World Camera Settings

    Height of 100

    Hero

    Size: 5x5

    Control: Follows the mouse, or controlled by keyboard controls (M toggles between the two)

    Keyboard Control:
  • W gradually increases the Hero's speed
  • S gradually decreases the Hero's speed
  • A rotates the hero counter-clockwise
  • D rotates the hero clockwise

    • Rotation Speed: 45 degrees/second

    Initial Speed: 20 unites/second

    Space-Bar: Spawns an egg-projectile at a rate of one egg every second

    Spawned Egg

    Size: 1x1

    Direction: Aligned with Hero direction at time of launch

    Speed: 40 units/second

    Expiration:
  • Passes the Bounds of the World
  • At Collision with Enemy

    • Enemy

    Size: 5x5

    Number of Enemies: 10

    Location: Randomly spawned within 90% of the world boundaries

    Expiration:
  • At Collision with Hero
  • At 4th Collision with an Egg
  • Each egg costs 80% of Enemy's Current Energy

    • Spawning: A new enemy is spawned so that the number and location conditions are always met

    Application Status

    The application prints out the following:
  • Hero:
  • Control mode: mouse / keyboard
  • Number of times the hero touched the enemy
  • Egg:
  • Number of eggs currently in the world
  • Enemy:
  • Total current enemy count
  • Total number destroyed
  • Q-key:
  • Quits the application

    • World Camera Settings

    Height of 100

    Hero

    Size: 5x5

    Control: Follows the mouse, or controlled by keyboard controls (M toggles between the two)

    Keyboard Control:
  • W gradually increases the Hero's speed
  • S gradually decreases the Hero's speed
  • A rotates the hero counter-clockwise
  • D rotates the hero clockwise

    • Rotation Speed: 45 degrees/second

    Initial Speed: 20 unites/second

    Space-Bar: Spawns an egg-projectile at a rate of one egg every second

    Spawned Egg

    Size: 1x1

    Direction: Aligned with Hero direction at time of launch

    Speed: 40 units/second

    Expiration:
  • Passes the Bounds of the World
  • At Collision with Enemy
  • At Collision with Waypoint

    • Enemy

    Size: 5x5

    Number of Enemies: 10

    Location: Randomly spawned within 90% of the world boundaries

    Expiration:
  • At Collision with Hero
  • At 4th Collision with an Egg
  • Each egg costs 20% of Enemy's Current Energy

    • Spawning: A new enemy is spawned so that the number and location conditions are always met

    Movement:
  • Speed: 30 units/second
  • Travels between waypoints
  • Sequentially
  • Randomly
  • (J-key Toggles between the two)
  • When waypoint is respawned: plane adjusts it's route

    • Waypoint

    Size: 5x5

    Number of Waypoints: 6

    Waypoint Labels: A - F

    Expiration
  • At 4th Collision with Egg
  • Each egg costs 25% of health

    • Spawning: Each waypoint has a 30x30 spawning region surrounding their initial location

    H: hides the waypoints

    Application Status

    The application prints out the following:
  • Hero:
  • Control mode: mouse / keyboard
  • Number of times the hero touched the enemy
  • Egg:
  • Number of eggs currently in the world
  • Cooldown / Fire Ready
  • Enemy:
  • Total current enemy count
  • Total number destroyed
  • Q-key:
  • Quits the application
  • H-key:
  • Hides the Waypoints
  • J-key
  • Moves planes randomly or sequentially
